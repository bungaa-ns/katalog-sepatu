About Us

BNGSHOES.COM merged with PT Mitra Gaya Indah in 2024, with the aim to serve sophisticated and trendy consumers who want something “different”. BNGSHOES.COM provides a wide selection of fashion shoes from unique and unusual international brands: Schutz, Arezzo, Pretty Ballerina, Gaimo, Unisa, Franco & Co, Mario Fagni, Kontatto, and Paciotti.

Now through BNGSHOES.COM, we present a wider collection of fashion shoes and sandals from well-known global brands such as Birkenstock, Dr. Martens, Staccato, Steve Madden, Nine West, Clarks, Rockport, and Fitflop. Not only fashion shoes, BNGSHOES also offers a wide range of fashion bags for various events, from parties, business events, or just socializing with friends and family.

Our mission is to provide you with a convenient and seamless online shopping experience. Customers can visit our experience-based stores, have orders delivered to their homes, pick up orders at selected stores and provide free shipping throughout Indonesia, so with just one click, BNGSHOES is already at your doorstep.
